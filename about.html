about me.

I am an undergraduate student at the University of Colorado Boulder with a major in Strategic Communications with an emphasis in Media Design and a minor in Creative Technology and Design.

During college, I have developed a passion for design. Whether that was sketching a rough draft for a logo design on my iPad or finalizing a mockup on Photoshop, I have learned to appreciate the process of designing concepts.
