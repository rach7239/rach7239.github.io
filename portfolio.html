Product Design

For this project, I redesigned a logo to create a more efficient logo for the company. I utilized different design programs, including Photoshop and Illustrator to complete this project.

Logo Design Sketches and Digital Iterations

For this branding project, I learned the importance of the initial sketching and iteration steps of a design process. For the first part of this process, I used Procreate to sketch different concepts on my iPad. After this, I switched over to Illustrator to create 50 digital iterations to refine my previous sketches.

Design Mockups

After finalizing the logo for the branding project, I created mockups for physical merchandise. This was one of the most exciting parts of the design process because I was able to see my digital designs come to life.
